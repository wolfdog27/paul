The Eleven Series

In numerology, the number eleven is known as the intuitive master. How do you align with your portal of supreme balance? In this workshop, we will navigate the polarity and merge the bridge between above and below.
The workshop includes:
  • grounding mediation aligning the chakras
  • exploration of the astrological energies of the current moment
  • consultation of the tarot
  • ending the workshop with a little movement to imbue the lessons learned.
This workshop will be held eleven times, on the 11th of each month from April to February!
