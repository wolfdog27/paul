A Brief History

I’m a native of Everett, Washington. Currently residing in the NYC. I was born on the 12th of March, which makes me a Pisces rising, Aries moon, and Pisces sun, AKA I’m sensible! At 18 I moved to New York City to further my Musical Theatre education at the American Musical and Dramatic Academy. Post graduation, I booped and bopped around the continent doing things . . . that are on my resume ;) Then in 2011 I got hit by a taxi resulting in a broken ankle and consequently move back to the hometown. What I thought was the most devastating episode to ever happen to me ended up being the most incredible.

Amazing Things Happen When You Are Stripped From Your Existence.

The intention post healing was to move back to NYC ASAP. However bigger forces were at play. I didn’t realize that the taxi accident was the cattails to the biggest transformation of my life. Here’s the timeline in bullet points:

  • Received my Yoga and NASM certifications
  • Ended up performing at Seattle’s top theatrical institutions
  • Got to be involved in the inception of The Studios. (A Center for the performing arts - Training Center + Studio Rental + Performance Space)
  • Met incredible humans that have changed my life forever
  • Started to understand the power of spiritual energy
  • Started teaching dance and musical theatre classes to all levels. (5th grade thru senior citizens)
  • Met the man of my dreams
  • Healed my relationship with my family
  • Started A Sensible Theatre Company with Jared Michael Brown!
  • Creatively consulted and appeared in The Millennial Experience - Directed by my man!
  • Started directing and choreographing Musicals
  • Produced and performed in Pageant the Musical via ASTC
  • Got engaged to the most stunning man ever!
  • Moved back to the NYC with said Fiancé
  • Starting teaching at my alma mater
  • Discovered my gift as an Astrology | Tarot channeler
  • Realized what it truly means to love myself.

So . . . as I’m typing this I’m realizing that this sounds like quite the fun plot for a book - maybe one day - but I digress. The journey has been astronomically stunning, challenging, humbling, enlightening, disbelieving, freeing, and I could not be more grateful for every moment of it.

So Fleehop-Falap-Ball-Change your way thru my website. Sending Love!
